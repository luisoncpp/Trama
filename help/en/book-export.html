Book Exporting

When you are ready to publish or share your manuscript, Trama compiles and exports your chapters under the book/ directory into various standard target formats including Markdown, HTML, Word (DOCX), EPUB, and PDF.

How to Access

Open the Import and export panel from the sidebar rail (the transfer icon), then scroll to the Book export section. Choose a format from the dropdown and click Export Book.

Example Workflow

To generate a final manuscript PDF:

  1. Open the Import and export sidebar panel.
  2. Under Book export, choose PDF (.pdf) from the format dropdown.
  3. Click Export Book.
  4. In the dialog, specify the output filename, directory location, title, and author.
  5. Trama merges the chapters from your book/ directory in corkboard order, applies clean typography stylesheets, and writes the complete document to the destination path.
