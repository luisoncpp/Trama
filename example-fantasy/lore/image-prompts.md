# Image Generation Prompts (GPT Image 2)

These prompts are specifically optimized for GPT Image 2, utilizing its ability to handle text generation, negative constraints, and precise, grounded details. 

## 🎭 Characters

### Cael — The Bard
**Prompt:**
> Atmospheric dark fantasy concept art. A portrait of Cael, a quiet, observant traveling bard in his mid-20s. He is standing on a dirt road at dusk. He wears practical, travel-worn clothing: an old tunic, a faded cloak, and boots that look uncomfortable. Slung across his back is a battered lute secured inside a rigid, reinforced leather travel case. At his belt is a simple, small knife meant for slicing bread and cheese. His expression is wary, calculating, and tired, not heroic. He holds a scrap of parchment with the text "The Weight of Three" written in elegant script. Muted, realistic lighting. 
> 
> **Negative constraints:** NO shiny armor, NO swords, NO magical glowing effects, NO heroic action poses, NO bright colors, NO clean clothes.

### Maren — The Baker
**Prompt:**
> Grounded dark fantasy realism. Maren, a practical, capable woman in her late 30s. She is standing near a wooden travel cart, holding a round loaf of rustic crusty bread. She wears sturdy, practical traveling clothes and a thick wool cloak. Her face shows years of hard work, grief, and absolute no-nonsense competence. Her expression is direct, sharp, and pragmatic, analyzing the viewer. In the background, sacks of flour are visible in her cart. Cinematic lighting, earthy tones.
> 
> **Negative constraints:** NO delicate dresses, NO weapons, NO magical elements, NO damsel-in-distress tropes, NO excessive makeup, NO fantasy tavern wenches. 

### Oswin — The Novice Scribe
**Prompt:**
> Realistic medieval fantasy illustration. Oswin, a 19-year-old nervous but earnest scribe. He is clutching a thick, heavy leather-bound journal to his chest. He wears the simple, unadorned robes of a monastic copyist. Slung over his shoulder is a waterproof satchel containing stoppered glass ink pots. His fingers are stained with dark iron gall ink. He is looking at a page in his journal where the text "Speak the true name" is written perfectly in tiny, flawless calligraphy, surrounded by a dark drawn box. His eyes are wide, observant, and slightly anxious. 
> 
> **Negative constraints:** NO wizard robes, NO magical staves, NO glowing runes, NO heroic confidence, NO combat gear, NO ancient white beards.

### Aldren — The King
**Prompt:**
> Somber, moody dark fantasy portrait. Aldren, a human king in his late 40s, sitting alone in a cold, austere stone receiving room. He looks deeply tired, carrying the weight of a 20-year lie. He wears practical but high-quality regal clothing in subdued colors, with a simple circlet indicating his status. His face shows genuine grief mixed with the exhaustion of maintaining absolute control. The lighting is dim, coming from a single window facing south, casting long shadows. He is holding a piece of parchment.
> 
> **Negative constraints:** NO evil sneers, NO glowing red eyes, NO massive golden crowns, NO opulent jewel-encrusted thrones, NO villainous spikes, NO triumphant poses.

### Corvin — The Prince / The Demon King
**Prompt:**
> Chilling psychological horror fantasy art. A young man in his 20s sitting perfectly still on a heavy, rough-hewn stone throne in a freezing, vast chamber. He wears plain, immaculate clothing. His face is human and handsome, but completely hollowed out—his expression possesses a terrifying, mechanical precision and absolute, clinical indifference. There is no warmth or humanity in his eyes. The room is bathed in a sickly grey-green bioluminescent light. The architecture around him is slightly wrong, full of jagged, asymmetrical shadows.
> 
> **Negative constraints:** NO horns, NO red glowing eyes, NO monstrous limbs, NO typical demon features, NO fire, NO spikes, NO weapons, NO evil smirks or gloating expressions.

## 🗺️ Places

### Valdenmere — The Capital
**Prompt:**
> Wide landscape concept art of a medieval administrative city on flat ground at the confluence of two muddy rivers. The city is a chaotic accumulation of function over beauty: old stone walls added onto walls, irregular districts, and winding ancient streets. The sky is overcast and grey. In the northern quarter stands a massive, looming stone palace that looks cold and utilitarian rather than beautiful. The atmosphere is grounded, realistic, and unmagical. 
> 
> **Negative constraints:** NO glowing magical spires, NO floating castles, NO perfectly symmetrical architecture, NO bright sunny skies, NO towering fantastical cathedrals.

### The Hollow of Drest
**Prompt:**
> Eerie, atmospheric folk-horror landscape. An isolated, claustrophobic medieval village locked away in a dense, suffocating forest of dark pine trees. It is the dead of night. Every single window in the village is tightly shuttered and every door is barred. At the edge of the treeline stands a single, carved wooden offering post. The post is completely stripped bare. The shadows are long, sharp, and oppressive. 
> 
> **Negative constraints:** NO welcoming tavern lights, NO stars or moon, NO people visible, NO fairies, NO glowing magic, NO standard fantasy village cheerfulness.

### Solen's Crossing
**Prompt:**
> A bustling, prosperous medieval river town during a festival. A massive, wide cobbled stone bridge connects two banks of a broad, slow river. In the center of the bridge stands a stone statue of a warrior pointing north. The town features thick stone facades and rich merchant houses. Crimson and gold cloth banners are draped everywhere. One large banner prominently features the text "ALDRIC". The air is filled with the smoke of roasting fires. It looks aggressively wealthy and convinced of its own heroic history.
> 
> **Negative constraints:** NO poverty, NO ruins, NO grimdark lighting, NO rain, NO muddy slums.

### Monastery of the Bound Hand
**Prompt:**
> Stark, minimalist architectural fantasy art. A sprawling, low-profile fortress built horizontally across the face of a freezing, grey mountain peak. It looks like a pale stone scar against the rock. The architecture is utterly devoid of ornamentation, built for absolute endurance against the wind. In the foreground, a windowless interior stone corridor lit by pale iron sconces. Monks in identical woolen robes are walking with terrifying, clockwork synchronization.
> 
> **Negative constraints:** NO tall steeples, NO stained glass windows, NO gold decorations, NO warmth, NO chaotic movement, NO magical auras.

### The Osswall and The Verge
**Prompt:**
> Surreal, oppressive dark fantasy landscape. A vast, completely featureless flatland covered in unnatural, dark, almost black grass under a sky that presses down like a heavy grey lid. In the distance rises an impossibly massive black granite fortress that looks like a constructed mountain range. The fortress has haphazardly clustered towers with wrong, unsettling proportions. A massive open archway yawns at the base. Above the gate hangs a limp dark flag bearing a stark, jagged geometric symbol.
> 
> **Negative constraints:** NO mountains in the background, NO trees, NO rolling hills, NO traditional medieval castle designs, NO colorful flags, NO sunlight.
